Campusnexus:Enhancing Student Support Throught Intelligent Chatbot

CampusNexus is an AI-powered chatbot designed to streamline and enhance student support services within educational institutions. By leveraging artificial intelligence, natural language processing (NLP), and interactive dialogue systems, CampusNexus provides quick, accurate, and round-the-clock responses to common campus-related queries such as admission procedures, course details, faculty information, fee structure, and event schedules.The system is developed using technologies such as Python, AIML (Artificial Intelligence Markup Language), Faiss for semantic search, and Streamlit for a user-friendly interface. CampusNexus reduces the burden on administrative staff while ensuring students receive timely support, contributing to a more efficient and tech-enabled campus experience. The project aims to demonstrate how intelligent virtual assistants can improve student engagement, simplify information access, and foster smart campus automation in the era of digital transformation.
